Rolling Fabric Blind

A ¾” dowel serves as the rod for the blind.The 3D-printed gears have flanges to prevent the belt from slipping off. Design 1 of the gears correspond to the two attached to the dowel. Design 2 of the gears is attached to the stepper motors, which are mounted to the housing unit. The distance between the two pull the belt taut and provide enough tension to rotate the dowel and curtain of vinyl mesh attached.

Materials:

  • 3D-printed gears
  • ¾” dowel
  • Rubber belt
  • Stepper Motor
  • Vinyl Mesh
