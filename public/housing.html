Housing Unit


Physical Component Construction


Constructed with red oak wood, the unit is composed of two shelves that hold a total of for key dishes. The back panel of the unit houses the electrical circuit, including the Raspberry Pi.

  • Housing box dimensions: 11.75”x20”x12.5”
  • Stands 5.25” off the base of housing box

    • Materials

  • Red oak wood (5.5” wide, 0.75” thick)
  • Dark walnut varnish
  • Polyurethane coating
  • Wood and cabinet screws
  • 1.5” deck screws
  • 0.5” Phillips screws (to mount motors)
