Smart Key Rack Engineering Project

Theta Tau Epsilon Chi Class



Dynamic Components


Pressure-detecting doormat

Stepping on the doormat triggers the rolling fabric blinds of the key rack to open, revealing the key rack. By employing a functionality prompted by the passive act of stepping on the doormat, the motion of the key rack will remind unwary residents to take their keys on the way out.

Smart key rack

Fabric blinds will hide the key rack and its contents when the product is not in use. When a resident steps on the doormat, the blinds will function similar to a window shade, rolling up to reveal the five key dishes. Each resident will be assigned a key dish on the rack. From there, pressure detectors at the bottom of each key dish will track the presence of each resident’s keys.

Website displaying who's at home

A Raspberry Pi computer will provide real-time updates monitoring presence of each resident. This information will be displayed on a simple website that residents will be able to access remotely.



Mechanical Components


Rolling Fabric Blinds

The fabric blind covering the keys rolls up when the pressure plate is depressed, passively reminding the users to grab their keys on the way out or to rack them on the way in.

Motor Functionality

The Raspberry Pi will be connected to the motors through a L298N motor driver. The motor driver accepts an external 12V DC power supply adapted from a power outlet. The 5V output of the Raspberry Pi will be used to control the motor driver. Upon detecting pressure on the door mat, the blind will open if it is not already open. The rotational speed of the motors will be adjusted by modifying the pulse width modulation (PWM) such that the blind rolls as fast as possible without swinging. The motors will then rotate for a fixed time until the blind is completely open or closed.

Blind Mechanism

Aluminum gears are attached to the 12V DC motors on each end of the housing. These gears turn a drive belt that in turn spins aluminum gears on the 3/4” dowel, which in turn rolls up the fabric shade.
